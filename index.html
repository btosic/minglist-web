Privacy Policy

The company Minglist d.o.o. is determined to adhere to all applicable rules and laws, including but not limited to, data protection rules.
With respect to compliance with the personal data laws, kindly note that we do not collect, store or process in any other way any of your personal data, or track your usage of our application in any way.
General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”) imposes the obligation to inform the data subjects whose personal data are processed about how their personal data are handled and processed in general by the data controller. This information is contained in the legal document called privacy policy. However, when there is no processing of the personal data, there is consequently no such obligation.
